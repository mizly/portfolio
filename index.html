jarvis.diy

uOttaHack 7 | January 2025

Powered by Blackberry QNX, JARVIS is an innovative integration of physical and digital processing to produce 3D views from 2D spatial data.

JARVIS provides a cost efficient solution that translates images/videos from ANY camera into web-ready 3D models without expensive equipment, using machine learning and Gaussian Splatting.

TRACY

QHacks | February 2024

TRACY took 3rd place at QHacks 2024 by combining computer vision and machine learning to analyze tennis gameplay.

Using React.js for dynamic visuals, the project helps athletes improve by providing trajectory graphs from a monocular viewpoint and providing AI-driven insights, making a mark in sports tech innovation.
