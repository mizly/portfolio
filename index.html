TRACY took 3rd place at QHacks 2024 by combining computer vision and machine learning to analyze tennis gameplay.

Melodica

McGill AI Hacks | October 2023

Awarded Best Hack for All Arts, Melodica is a musician's Swiss Army knife, providing various functionalities for musicians.

After uploading an audio file, Melodica automatically separates and visualizes distinct instrumentals and audio tracks. A real-time and interactive audio player allows for individual track manipulation by the users.
